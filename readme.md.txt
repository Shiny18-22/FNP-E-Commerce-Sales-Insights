# FNP Sales Dashboard (Excel)

This project provides a comprehensive Excel-based analysis of e-commerce sales for Ferns N Petals (FNP), India’s leading gifting platform. The dataset includes over 1,000 records covering customer profiles, orders, and product information across multiple regions and occasions from January 2023 to early 2024.

## Key Features
- Detailed sales and revenue insights by occasion, category, and region.
- Identification of top-performing products and high-revenue occasions.
- Seasonality and buying pattern analysis by month, week, and day.
- Operational metrics including average delivery time and reliability.
- Uses advanced Excel functions like PivotTables, VLOOKUP, calculated columns, and multi-sheet integration.

## Business Insights and Findings
- Highest revenue driven by key occasions such as Anniversary (₹674,634), Raksha Bandhan (₹631,585), and Holi (₹574,682).
- Leading product categories by revenue are Colors (₹1,005,645), Soft Toys (₹740,831), and Sweets (₹733,842).
- Strong demand observed from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities with significant order volumes in places like Bhatpara, Bidhannagar, Dhanbad, and Imphal.
- Buying behavior shows revenue spikes aligning with major festivals like Raksha Bandhan, Diwali, Holi, and Valentine’s Day.
- Consistent average delivery times around 5.5 days throughout the year, demonstrating operational reliability even during high-volume festive periods.

## Recommendations
- Expand marketing and logistical focus on Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities to harness growth potential in these emerging markets.
- Increase inventory and promotional activities for top-performing categories such as Colors and Soft Toys ahead of peak occasions like Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan.
- Maintain operational flexibility and efficiency during festival seasons to ensure reliable delivery and customer satisfaction.
